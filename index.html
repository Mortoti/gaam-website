Who We Are

About GAAM

The Ghana Association of Assembly Members is a unified body dedicated to enhancing local governance and driving community development. As the voice of assembly members across the nation, we advocate for their rights, provide capacity-building opportunities, and promote effective leadership at the grassroots level.

Through advocacy, collaboration, and capacity building, GAAM plays a crucial role in promoting good governance, transparency, and sustainable development.

Government Begins Payment of Assembly Members

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

GAAM is dedicated to advocating for the welfare of assembly members, promoting local governance, and ensuring effective community development across Ghana. We provide capacity-building, networking, and advocacy on behalf of all elected assembly members.

Membership is open to all elected assembly members in Ghana. Interested individuals can contact their regional representatives or reach out via the contact form on our About Us page for registration details.

GAAM provides advocacy, training, and networking opportunities for assembly members. With the recent government policy, members now also receive financial remuneration for their service — a long overdue recognition of their dedication.

The official website of the Ghana Association of Assembly Members was developed under the leadership of Hon. Frank Nelson Ackah. It was designed and developed by Mortoti-Agogyi Jephthah Lorlornyo, the founder of NOLAP Technologies.
